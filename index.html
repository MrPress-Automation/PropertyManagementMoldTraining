Curriculum

What Your Team Will Learn

Four hours of focused, practical training broken into easy-to-complete modules.

  1. 01

    Mold Fundamentals & Health Effects

    What mold is, how it grows, and the health effects associated with exposure for tenants and staff.

  2. 02

    Moisture Sources & Building Vulnerabilities

    Common water intrusion points in residential and commercial properties, including roofs, plumbing, HVAC, and building envelopes.

  3. 03

    Prevention & Routine Maintenance

    Proactive practices that stop mold before it starts, including humidity control and inspection schedules.

  4. 04

    Recognizing & Assessing Mold Growth

    How to visually identify suspected mold and determine when further assessment or remediation is required.

  5. 05

    Remediation Basics & Safe Response

    Overview of remediation procedures, PPE considerations, and when to escalate to certified remediation professionals.

  6. 06

    OSHA & EPA Guidelines

    How federal OSHA and EPA guidance applies to property management operations and staff responsibilities.

  7. 07

    Tenant Communication & Documentation

    Best practices for logging complaints, communicating with residents, and maintaining a defensible paper trail.
